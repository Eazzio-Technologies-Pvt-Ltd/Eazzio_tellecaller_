Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to standard questions about our auto-dialer and CRM.

What is a SIM-based auto dialer? ▼
A SIM-based auto dialer connects your web-admin panel to a mobile app on the caller's phone. It places outbound calls directly utilizing the mobile SIM card's calling pack rather than expensive VoIP charges, allowing you to run unlimited calling campaigns cheaply.
How do we sync leads from our CRM? ▼
Eazzio supports lead sync directly from Google Sheets or standard CRM systems using webhook API endpoints. You can also import leads directly via CSV uploads inside the admin panel.
Can we record calls placed by agents? ▼
Yes. The mobile calling application automatically records conversations and uploads the audio logs securely to your Eazzio web dashboard for review, quality assurance, and training.
Is there a calling limit on SIM cards? ▼
The daily calling limits are determined entirely by your mobile carrier plan (e.g. Jio, Airtel, Vodafone). Eazzio FAST itself imposes no limits on the number of automated calls placed.
